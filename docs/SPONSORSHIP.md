Sponsorship does not grant project ownership, maintainer access, voting rights,
release authority, access to private source, guaranteed support, or guaranteed
acceptance or priority for a feature request. Commercial support and enterprise
agreements remain separate from community sponsorship.

## Reporting A Problem

Report a broken or suspicious funding destination through the public support or
security process. Do not include payment details, account credentials, or other
private financial information in a public issue.
