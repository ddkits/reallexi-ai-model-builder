## EU And EEA Checklist

Treat prompts, uploads, training data, analytics, and support records as
potentially regulated data. Review lawful basis, purpose limitation, retention,
deletion, processor/controller roles, data location, and whether GDPR, the EU AI
Act, or sector rules apply.

* European Commission data protection framework:
  https://commission.europa.eu/law/law-topic/data-protection/legal-framework-eu-data-protection_en
* European Commission AI regulatory framework:
  https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/regulatory-framework-ai

## USA Checklist

Review federal, state, sector, consumer-protection, privacy, security, and
contract obligations. Keep product claims truthful, protect credentials and
customer data, document retention, and maintain an incident response process.

* FTC privacy and security guidance:
  https://www.ftc.gov/business-guidance/privacy-security
* United States Copyright Office overview:
  https://www.copyright.gov/what-is-copyright/

## Data And Model Licenses

Public availability does not automatically permit commercial use. Review every
model, embedding model, adapter, and dataset card. Complete provider terms or
access requests before using gated resources.

## Debug Sharing

Diagnostic sharing must be opt-in, clearly described, and limited to the data
needed for support. Remove credentials, customer content, and private model or
dataset material before sharing any report.
