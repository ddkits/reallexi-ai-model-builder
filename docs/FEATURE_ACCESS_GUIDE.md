## Community Features

* local dashboard, training service, launcher, health checks, and local Admin;
* model catalog, research guidance, LoRA and supported full-model training;
* phased continuation, checkpoints, recovery, evaluation, and editable recipes;
* curated/manual sources, bounded loading, references, and provenance evidence;
* original artifacts, lineage, inspection, SafeTensors from the trained job,
  and GGUF Q8/F16;
* token-local Hugging Face identity, inventory, namespaces, and access checks;
* public extension manifests, themes, pages, hooks, routes, and contribution
  contracts.

Community workflows remain usable during a shop outage. Premium actions fail
closed without disabling local training or standard exports.

## Commercial Studios

Image Model and LoRA, Audio and Voice, Video Generation, and 3D Asset studios
are independently entitled shop-managed plugins with shared reviewed runtimes.
Current price and bundle terms must be confirmed on the active shop listing.

Private connectors, managed deployment operations, customer integrations, and
hosted support require Enterprise access or a written agreement. Shared runtime
dependencies never grant product access by themselves.

For purchases, the active product page, checkout summary, agreement, and
provider receipt control current availability, price, term, and refund
conditions.
